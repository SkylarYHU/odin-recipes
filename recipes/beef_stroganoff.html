Classic Beef Stroganoff

This Beef Stroganoff recipe is a comforting and creamy dish featuring tender beef strips cooked in a rich mushroom and sour cream sauce. It's best served over egg noodles or rice for a hearty and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Beef sirloin
  • Butter
  • Onion
  • Mushrooms
  • Garlic
  • Beef broth
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Sour cream
  • Flour
  • Egg noodles
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Prepare the beef: Slice the beef sirloin into thin strips and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook the beef: In a skillet, melt butter over medium-high heat and sear the beef until browned. Remove and set aside.
  3. Saut√© the vegetables: In the same pan, cook the onions, mushrooms, and garlic until softened.
  4. Make the sauce: Stir in flour, then add beef broth and Worcestershire sauce. Simmer until the sauce thickens.
  5. Finish with sour cream: Reduce heat and stir in sour cream until smooth. Add the cooked beef back to the pan.
  6. Cook the noodles: Boil egg noodles in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  7. Serve: Spoon the beef stroganoff over egg noodles and garnish with fresh parsley. Enjoy!
